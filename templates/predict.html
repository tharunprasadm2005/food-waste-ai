AI Prediction Overview

The system analyzes historical demand, event size, food type, preparation method, and seasonality to estimate excess food. The predicted wastage is influenced by pricing strategy, guest turnout uncertainty, and seasonal demand variation. Adjusting portion size and pricing dynamically can reduce waste. One-third of the food produced globally is wasted, while millions go hungry. Smart prediction enables timely redistribution and sustainable food management.

What-If Waste Reduction Simulation

If food quantity is reduced by 10%, potential waste reduction could be approximately 5–8% without affecting service quality. This system uses ensemble machine learning models. CatBoost is prioritized due to its superior handling of categorical features and higher prediction stability.
