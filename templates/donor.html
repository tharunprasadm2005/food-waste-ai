Donation Information & Metrics

Millions face hunger while excess food is wasted daily. Your contribution helps reduce waste, hunger, and environmental impact.Beyond the immediate social benefit, your donation plays a vital role in global sustainability. Currently, food waste is responsible for approximately 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions; if it were a country, it would be the third-largest emitter in the world. By redirecting surplus meals, you are actively preventing the decay of organic matter in landfills, which releases potent methane gas, and ensuring that the water and energy used in production do not go to waste.
